Welcome to Battleship!

This is the classic game of battleship. If you have never heard of the game, here is a link to read: Battleship Here is how to play:

  • After hitting the start button, you will be picking the start and end of your ships. Follow the instructions provided on the left
    • As of right now, please don't have any part of the ship touching another ship
  • Once the ships are place, the game will begin
  • Click on the enemy's board to search for the enemy's ships
    • The ships left to find and their size are provided at the top of the screen
  • The first one to find all the ships wins!

Good luck!
